# Three Point Rectangle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three Point Rectangle' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a rectangle specified by three points.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-rectangle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the start point of one side of the rectangle.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> [Specify length a numerical value](./sketching-tips#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Add edges to the position of the curve to be created](./sketching-tips#add-edges-to-the-position-of-the-curve-to-be-created).
   - <kbd>X</kbd>, <kbd>Y</kbd>, <kbd>Z</kbd> **X, Y, Z-axis:** [Limit to the coordinate axis direction](./sketching-tips#limit-to-the-coordinate-axis-direction).
3. Click at the endpoint of the same side of the rectangle.
4. Click at the third point of the rectangle.
5. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
6. A rectangle is created.
