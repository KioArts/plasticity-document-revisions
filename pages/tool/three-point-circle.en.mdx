# Three-Point Circle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three-Point Circle' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a circle specified by three points on the circumference.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-three-point-circle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click on the first point on the circumference.
3. Move the cursor and click on the second point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Add edges to the position of the curve to be created](./sketching-tips#add-edges-to-the-position-of-the-curve-to-be-created).
   - <kbd>X</kbd>, <kbd>Y</kbd>, <kbd>Z</kbd> **X, Y, Z-axis:** [Limit to the coordinate axis direction](./sketching-tips#limit-to-the-coordinate-axis-direction).
4. Move the cursor and click on the third point on the circumference.
5. A circle is created.
