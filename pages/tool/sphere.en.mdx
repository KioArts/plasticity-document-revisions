# Sphere

<Reference shortcut='' command='Sphere' button='Toolbar' />

Allows you to create a sphere specified by its center and diameter.

1. Click the button on the toolbar.
2. Click at the point for the sphiere's center.
3. Move the cursor and click at the desired radius.
   - Refer to [Solid Tool Common options](common-options) and [Solid Editing Boolean options](boolean-options) for other options.
4. Click OK in the command dialog or right-click to confirm.
5. A sphere is created.
