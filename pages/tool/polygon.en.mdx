# Regular Polygon

<Reference shortcut='' command='Polygon' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a regular polygon.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-regular-polygon" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the point for the polygon's center.
3. Move the cursor and click on a point on the circumscribed circle.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> [Specifythe diameter of the circumscribed circle with a numerical value](./sketching-tips#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>Shift</kbd> + Wheel up **Add a vertex:** Increases the vertices of the polygon.
   - <kbd>Shift</kbd> + Wheel down **Subtract a vertex:** Reduces the vertices of the polygon.
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Use Knife Mode.](./sketching-tips#use-knife-mode) 
   - <kbd>X</kbd>, <kbd>Y</kbd> **X, Y-axis:** [Constrain to the X or Y-axis](./sketching-tips#constrain-to-the-x-y-or-z-axis).
4. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
5. A regular polygon is created.

<Callout type="info" emoji="ðŸ’¡">
   The vertices of the polygon can be increased or decreased using the up and down keys.
</Callout>
