# Center Circle 

<Reference shortcut="" command="Center Circle" button="Toolbar" />

You can create a circle by specifying the center and the diameter.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-center-circle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the point for the circle's center.
3. Move the cursor and click on a point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> [Specifythe diameter with a numerical value](./sketching-tips#specify-dimensions-numerically).
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** [Add edges to the position of the curve to be created](./sketching-tips#add-edges-to-the-position-of-the-curve-to-be-created).
   - <kbd>V</kbd> **Vertical/ Horizontal:** Changes the inclination with respect to the construction plane.
   - <kbd>X</kbd>, <kbd>Y</kbd> **X, Y-axis:** [Limit to the coordinate axis direction](./sketching-tips#limit-to-the-coordinate-axis-direction).
4. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
5. A circle is created.
