# Center Circle 

<Reference shortcut="" command="Center Circle" button="Toolbar" />

You can create a circle by specifying the center and diameter.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-center-circle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click at the point for the circle's center.
3. Move the cursor and click on a point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>Tab</kbd> 
      - Specify diameter with a numerical value.
   <Option_K />
   - <kbd>V</kbd> **Vertical/ Horizontal**
      - Changes the inclination with respect to the construction plane.
   <Option_X />
   <Option_Y />
4. Confirm with OK in the command dialog or right click.
5. A circle is created.
