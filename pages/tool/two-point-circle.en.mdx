# Two-Point Circle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Two-Point Circle' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a circle specified by two points of the diameter.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-two-point-circle" /> on the toolbar.
2. Click on the first point on the circumference.
3. Move the cursor and click on the second point on the circumference.
   - <kbd>V</kbd> **Vertical/ Horizontal:** Changes the inclination with respect to the construction plane.
   - <kbd>K</kbd> **Knife:** Add edges to the face with the created curve.
   - <kbd>X</kbd> **X axis:** Limit to the X-axis direction.
   - <kbd>Y</kbd> **Y axis:** Limit to the Y-axis direction.
   - <kbd>Z</kbd> **Z axis:** Limit to the Z-axis direction.
4. A circle is created.
