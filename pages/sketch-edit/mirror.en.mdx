# Mirror

You can mirror curves.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut="Alt+X" command="Mirror" button="Command Bar Bottom Left" />

1. Select the curve to be mirrored.
2. Press the shortcut `Alt`+`X` or click the icon on the bottom left of the Command Bar.
3. Specify the mirroring direction and options:
   - `X`: **Position X**: Mirror in the positive direction of the X-axis.
   - `Y`: **Position Y**: Mirror in the positive direction of the Y-axis.
   - `Z`: **Position Z**: Mirror in the positive direction of the Z-axis.
   - `Shift`+`X`: **Negative X**: Mirror in the negative direction of the X-axis.
   - `Shift`+`Y`: **Negative Y**: Mirror in the negative direction of the Y-axis.
   - `Shift`+`Z`: **Negative Z**: Mirror in the negative direction of the Z-axis.
   - `F`: **Freestyle**: Mirror using the axis specified by the first click as the start point and the second click as the end point.
   - `Q`: **Union (merge)**: {/* Merge the two mirrored entities. */} {/* ??? unknown function */}
   - **Cut**: {/* Divide the mirror target with the mirror plane before mirroring. */} {/* ??? unknown function */}
4. Confirm with **OK** in the command dialog or right click.
