# Place

<Reference shortcut="Ctrl-D" command="Place" button="Left of Command Bar" />

You can duplicate curves, solids, sheets based on a specified axis.

1. Select the objects you want to duplicate.
2. Press the shortcut `Ctrl-D` or click the button.
3. Click on the reference point of the object to be duplicated.
4. Click on the reference point of the destination for duplication.
    - `S` **Scale**
        - Allows scaling.
    - `A` **Angle**
        - Allows rotation.
    - `D` **Offset**
        - Allows consecutive duplication.
    - `F` **Flip**
        - Allows flipping the duplication axis.
    - `X` **x-up**
        - Sets the duplication axis to the X-axis.
    - `Y` **y-up**
        - Sets the duplication axis to the Y-axis.
    - `Z` **z-up**
        - Sets the duplication axis to the Z-axis.
    - Other options: Refer to [Solid Editing Boolean options](boolean-options) .
5. Right-click to confirm.
6. \<Options\> To perform further duplications:
   1. Click on the reference point of the destination for duplication.
   2. Right-click to confirm.
7. Press the shortcut `Escape` to exit.
