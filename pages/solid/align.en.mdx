## Align

The **Align** command runs based on the selected object:
     - **Align Surface:** If two Edges are selected, see [**Align Surface**](align-surface).
     - **Align Vertex:** If two Vertices are selected or Curves are selected, see [**Align Vertex**](/sketch/align-vertex) in the Sketch Commands section.
