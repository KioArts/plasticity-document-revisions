## Description

Edit the surface of a Face or Solid by increasing surface Control Vertices(CV).

## Basic Usage

1. Select a face or solid.
2. Press <kbd>Shift + S</kbd> to raise the degree of the selected face or solid.
3. The surface CVs are increased.

<Callout type="info">
**Note:**
  - To hide surface CVs, use the [Toggle Points](/sketch/toggle-points) command from the Command Palette.
  - To select a row loop of CVs, press <kbd>Alt</kbd>.
  - If the surface has too many CVs, use the [Rebuild](/solid/rebuild) command to simplify it.
</Callout>
