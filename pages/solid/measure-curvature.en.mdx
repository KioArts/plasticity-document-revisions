# Measure Curvature

<Reference shortcut="" command="Measure Curvature" button="" />

Measure the curvature of surface edges, curves, and isoparms using a curvature comb (displayed in white) to visually evaluate shape quality and smoothness.

![Curvature Comb](/img/curvature-comb.png)

## Basic Usage

1. Select two adjacent and touching surface edges.
2. Type **"Measure Curvature"** in the Command Palette.
3. Set the options in the Command Dialog.
   - <Kst>Command Dialog</Kst>
         - **Scale:** Adjusts visual size of combs.
4. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.

<Callout type="info" emoji="ðŸ’¡">
**Info**    
Curvature evaluation helps you:
    - **Ensure surface quality:** Identify and eliminate ripples, dents, or unintended kinks to maintain smooth transitions.
    - **Verify continuity:** Assess whether surfaces meet with G0 (position), G1 (tangent), or G2 (curvature) continuity.
    - **Refine reflection flow:** Essential in automotive and industrial design, where surfaces must appear seamless under various lighting conditions.
    - **Predict manufacturability:** Sharp or abrupt curvature changes can pose challenges in machining, molding, or fabrication.
</Callout>
