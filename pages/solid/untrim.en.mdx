# Untrim

<Reference shortcut="Alt + T" command="Untrim" button="" />

Create a Sheet based on the original shape of the selected Face.

## Basic Usage

1. Select the Face you want to untrim.
2. Press <kbd>Alt + T</kbd>.
- <Kst>Command Dialog</Kst>
    - **Keep edges:** Choose whether to insert imprinted lines to preserve the edges of the trimmed surface.
- <Kst>Keyboard Shortcuts</Kst>
    - <kbd>Tab</kbd> **Keep edges:** Toggles the option to retain the edges of the trimmed surface.
3. A Sheet based on the original shape is created.
