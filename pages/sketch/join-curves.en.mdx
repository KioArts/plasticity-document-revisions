## Description

Join Curves at connecting points.

## Basic Usage

1. Select the Curves you want to join.
2. Press <kbd>J</kbd>.
3. Curves are joined.

<Callout emoji="❗">
**Note**<br />
To join two Curves, their endpoints must be perfectly aligned.
When they are perfectly aligned, the Points will turn blue-green. If they appear purple, they are not aligned. To ensure a precise alignment, see [Object Snaps](/plasticity-essentials/plasticity-interface/snap).
</Callout>
