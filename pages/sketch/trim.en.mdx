## Description

Remove specified segments from planar or 3D curves. Segments are defined by the intersections, endpoints, or control points that divide a curve into distinct sections.

## Basic Usage

1. Press <kbd>T</kbd>.
2. Click on the segment you want to trim.
3. The segment is removed.
