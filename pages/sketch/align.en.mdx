## Align

The **Align** command runs based on the selected object:
     - **Align Vertex:** If two Vertices are selected or Curves are selected, see [**Align Vertex**](align-vertex).
     - **Align Surface:** If two Edges are selected, see [**Align Surface**](/solid/align-surface) in the Solid Commands section.
