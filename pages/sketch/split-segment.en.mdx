# Split Segment

<Reference shortcut="" command="Split Segment" button="Toolbar" />

You can split a Curve into two segments.

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-split-segment" /> on the Toolbar.
2. Click on the point you want to split.
3. The Point is inserted.
