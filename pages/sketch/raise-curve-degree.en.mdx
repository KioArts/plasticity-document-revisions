# Raise Curve Degree

<Reference shortcut="Shift + S" command="Raise Degree" button="Right of Command Bar" buttonIcon="button-raise-degree-curve-face" />

Increase the number of Control Vertices(CVs) and number of Degrees on the selected Curves.

## Basic Usage

1. Select one or more curves.
2. Press <kbd>Shift + S</kbd> or click on the button <ButtonIcon type="button-raise-degree-curve-face"/> on the right side of the Command Bar to increase the degree of the curves.
