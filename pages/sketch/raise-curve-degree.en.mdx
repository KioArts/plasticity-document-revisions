## Description

Raise the degree of the selected curves. This automatically increases the number of control vertices (CVs) and may introduce extra spans, depending on how complex the curve's geometry is.

## Basic Usage

1. Select one or more curves.
2. Press <kbd>Shift + S</kbd> to increase the degree of the curve(s).
3. The curve(s) gain an extra degree, and the newly created CVs are automatically selected.

<Callout type="info" emoji="💡">
**Tip**: Use the [Toggle Points](/sketch/toggle-points) command to hide the control points of the selected curve. This is especially handy when the CV layout is densely packed and hard to navigate in Plasticity.
</Callout>
