# Insert Knot

<Reference shortcut="" command="Insert Knot" button="Toolbar" buttonIcon='button-insert-cv-into-curve' />

Insert a CV (Control-Vertex) in a spline curve.

## Basic Usage

1. Click the button <ButtonIcon type="button-split-segment" /> on the Toolbar.
2. Click on the point where you want to insert a CV.
3. A CV is inserted, adding additional point to the spline curve.

<VideoPlayer fileName="insert-knot"/>


<Callout type="info" emoji="🗒️">
**Note** Inserting a Control-Vertex requires the curve to be a spline; otherwise, no effect will occur on a regular segmented curve.     
</Callout>
