# Scale

You can scale solids or sheets.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut="S" command="Scale" button="Bottom-left of Command Bar" />

1. Select the solid or sheet you want to scale.
2. Press the shortcut `S` or click the icon at the bottom-left of the Command Bar.
3. Use the displayed Scale Gizmo to resize:
   - `X`: **X axis**: Restricts scaling to the X-axis.
   - `Y`: **Y axis**: Restricts scaling to the Y-axis.
   - `Z`: **Z axis**: Restricts scaling to the Z-axis.
   - `Shift`+`Z`: **Z plane**: Restricts scaling to the Z-plane.
   - `Shift`+`X`: **X plane**: Restricts scaling to the X-plane.
   - `Shift`+`Y`: **Y plane**: Restricts scaling to the Y-plane.
   - `S`: **Uniform**: Scales uniformly in the X, Y, and Z directions.
   - `F`: **Freestyle**: Specify the starting point with the first click and the ending point with the next click.
   - `V`: **Pivot**: Restricts scaling in the direction of the clicked object.
   - `W`: **World space**: Releases the scaling restrictions.
4. Confirm by clicking **OK** in the command dialog or right-clicking.
