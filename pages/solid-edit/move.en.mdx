# Move

<Reference shortcut="G" command="Move" button="Bottom-left of Command Bar" />

You can move solids, sheets, faces, and edges.

1. Select a solid, sheet, face, or edge as the object to move.
2. Press the shortcut `G` or click the button at the bottom-left of the Command Bar.
3. Use the displayed Move Gizmo to move.
   - `F` **Freestyle**
      - Click to specify the starting point and the endpoint, determining the direction and distance of movement.
   - `W` **World space**
      - Releases the movement restrictions.
   - Refer to [Solid Editing Common options](common-options) for other options.
4. Confirm with **OK** in the command dialog or right click.
