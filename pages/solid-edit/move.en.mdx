# Move

You can move solids, sheets, faces, and edges.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut="G" command="Move" button="Bottom-left of Command Bar" />

1. Select a solid, sheet, face, or edge as the object to move.
2. Press the shortcut `G` or click the icon at the bottom-left of the Command Bar.
3. Use the displayed Move Gizmo to move:
   - `X`: **X axis**: Restricts movement to the X-axis.
   - `Y`: **Y axis**: Restricts movement to the Y-axis.
   - `Z`: **Z axis**: Restricts movement to the Z-axis.
   - `Shift`+`Z`: **Z plane**: Restricts movement to the Z-plane.
   - `Shift`+`X`: **X plane**: Restricts movement to the X-plane.
   - `Shift`+`Y`: **Y plane**: Restricts movement to the Y-plane.
   - `G`: **Screen space**: Restricts movement to the visible plane.
   - `F`: **Freestyle**: Specify the starting point with the first click and the ending point with the next click.
   - `V`: **Pivot**: Restricts movement in the direction of the clicked object.
   - `W`: **World space**: Releases the movement restrictions.
4. Confirm by clicking **OK** in the command dialog or right-clicking.
