# Regular Polygon

This function allows you to create a regular polygon.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut='' command='Regular Polygon' button='Toolbar' />

1. Click on the icon in the toolbar.
2. Click at the center of the polygon.
3. Move the cursor and click on a point on the circumscribed circle.
   - `Tab` ï¼š Specify the diameter of the circumscribed circle numerically.
   - `Shift`+`Wheel up` : **Add a vertex** : Increases the vertices of the polygon.
   - `Shift`+`Wheel down` : **Subtract a vertex** : Reduces the vertices of the polygon.
   - `X` : **X axis** : Constrains to the X-axis direction.
   - `Y` : **Y axis** : Constrains to the Y-axis direction.
   - `Z` : **Z axis** : Constrains to the Z-axis direction.
4. Confirm by clicking **OK** in the command dialog or right-clicking.
5. The regular polygon is created.
