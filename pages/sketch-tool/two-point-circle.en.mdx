# Circle with Two Points

You can create a circle specified by two points of the diameter.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut='' command='Two-Point Circle' button='Toolbar' />

1. Click the icon on the toolbar.
2. Click at the first point of the diameter.
3. Move the cursor and click at the second point of the diameter.
   - `K` : **Knife** : Adds an edge to the face.
   - `V` : **Vertical/ Horizontal** : Changes the slope with respect to the construction plane.
   - `X` : **X axis** : Constrains to the X-axis.
   - `Y` : **Y axis** : Constrains to the Y-axis.
   - `Z` : **Z axis** : Constrains to the Z-axis.
4. A circle is created.
