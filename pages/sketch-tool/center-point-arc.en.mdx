# Arc from Center

This function allows you to create an arc by specifying the center and two points on the circumference.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut='' command='Center Point Arc' button='Toolbar' />

1. Click on the icon in the toolbar.
2. Click at the position for the circle's center.
3. Move the cursor and click at the first position for the diameter.
   - `X` : **X axis** : Limits to the X-axis direction.
   - `Y` : **Y axis** : Limits to the Y-axis direction.
   - `Z` : **Z axis** : Limits to the Z-axis direction.
4. Move the cursor and click at the second position on the circumference.
5. The arc is created.
