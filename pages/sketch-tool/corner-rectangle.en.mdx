# Rectangle from Diagonal

This function allows you to create a rectangle by specifying two points on the diagonal.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut='' command='Corner Rectangle' button='Toolbar' />

1. Click on the icon in the toolbar.
2. Click at the starting point of the diagonal of the rectangle.
   - `Tab` ï¼š Enter the length numerically.
   - `K` : **Knife** : Adds an edge to a face.
   - `C` : **Center / Corner** : Switches to Center Rectangle.
   - `S` : **Square** : Draws auxiliary lines for a square.
3. Click at the end point on the diagonal.
4. Confirm by clicking **OK** in the command dialog or right-clicking.
5. The rectangle is created.
