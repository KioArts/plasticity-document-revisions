# Three-Point Circle

<Reference shortcut='' command='Three-Point Circle' button='Toolbar' />

You can create a circle specified by three points on the circumference.

1. Click the button on the toolbar.
2. Click on the first point on the circumference.
3. Move the cursor and click on the second point on the circumference.
   - Refer to [Sketch Tool Common options](common-options) .
4. Move the cursor and click on the third point on the circumference.
5. A circle is created.
