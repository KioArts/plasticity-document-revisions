# Rectangle with Three Points

You can create a rectangle specified by three points.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut='' command='Three Point Rectangle' button='Toolbar' />

1. Click the icon on the toolbar.
2. Click at the start point of one side of the rectangle.
   - `Tab` : Specifies the length by a value.
   - `K` : **Knife** : Adds an edge to the face.
   - `X` : **X axis** : Constrains to the X-axis.
   - `Y` : **Y axis** : Constrains to the Y-axis.
   - `Z` : **Z axis** : Constrains to the Z-axis.
3. Click at the end point of the same side of the rectangle.
4. Click at the third point of the rectangle.
5. Confirm by clicking **OK** in the command dialog or by right-clicking.
6. A rectangle is created.
