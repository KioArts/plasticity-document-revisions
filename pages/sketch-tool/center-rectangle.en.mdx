# Rectangle from Center

This function allows you to create a rectangle by specifying the center point.

<KinouGaiyou shortcut='' command='Center Rectangle' button='Toolbar' />

1. Click on the icon in the toolbar.
2. Click at the position for the rectangle's center.
   - `Tab` : You can specify the length numerically.
   - `K` : **Knife** : Adds an edge to a face.
   - `C` : **Center / Corner** : Switches to Corner Rectangle.
   - `S` : **Square** : Limits to a square.
3. Click at a point on the diagonal.
4. Confirm by clicking **OK** in the command dialog or right-clicking.
5. The rectangle is created.
