## Displaying and Hiding Objects

You can temporarily hide or unhide Curves and solids.

- Hide selected: <kbd>H</kbd> 
- Hide unselected: <kbd>Shift-H</kbd>
- Unhide all: <kbd>Alt-H</kbd>
- Invert hidden: <kbd>Ctrl-H</kbd>
- Hide all Curves on the same plane: Select region and press <kbd>H</kbd>
