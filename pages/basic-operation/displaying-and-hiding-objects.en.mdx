## Displaying and Hiding Objects

You can temporarily hide or unhide curves and solids.

- Hide selected: `H` <YoutubeExternalIcon url="https://youtu.be/vgFxnePeY1g"/>
- Hide unselected: `Shift`+`H`
- Unhide all: `Alt`+`H`
- Invert hidden: `Ctrl`+`H`
- Hide all curves on the same plane: Select region and press `H` <YoutubeExternalIcon url="https://youtu.be/j0jyWmzZbpw"/>
