# Remove Material

<Reference shortcut="Alt + M" command="Remove Material" button="" />

You can remove the material from the solids, sheets, curves, and reference images.

1. Select the objects you want to remove the material from.
2. Press <kbd>Alt + M</kbd>.
3. The material will be reset to the initial value.
