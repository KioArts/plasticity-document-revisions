# Duplicate

<Reference shortcut="Shift-D" command="Duplicate" button="Left of Command Bar" />

You can duplicate: regions, Curves, Solids, Sheets, faces, edges.

1. Select the objects you want to duplicate.
2. Press <kbd>Shift-D</kbd> or click the button.
3. Use the displayed Move Gizmo to move.
   - <kbd>F</kbd> **Freestyle**
      - Click to specify the starting point and the endpoint, determining the direction and distance of movement.
   - Other options: Refer to [Common options](common-options) .
4. Confirm with OK in the command dialog or right click.

<Callout type="info">
   - When duplicating multiple regions, they will be duplicated as a combined shape.
</Callout>
