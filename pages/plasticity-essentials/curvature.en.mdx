# Curvature

The Curvature menu is located in the right side panel and provides direct control over the curvature and continuity comb display for the [Toggle Curvature](/sketch/toggle-curvature) and [Measure Continuity](/solid/measure-continuity) commands.

<Center 
width="400px">
![Curvature Menu](/img/curvature-menu.svg)
</Center>

## Adjusting Curvature Comb

To adjust the curvature comb, click and drag left or right, or manually enter a value in the Comb scale field (default: 1.00). 

<Center 
width="270px">
![Comb Scale](/img/comb-scale.png)
</Center>

## Adjusting Continuity Comb

For continuity analysis, the G0, G1, and G2 controls are located below the Comb scale field. The *Show maxes* option , available in Measure Continuity, enables the display of numerical deviation values and continuity indicators along the curve and edge.
